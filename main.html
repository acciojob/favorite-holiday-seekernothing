Matheran


Matheran is a picturesque hill station located in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra, India, renowned for its lush greenery, pleasant climate, and breathtaking views. Situated at an altitude of about 800 meters, it is Asia's only automobile-free hill station, offering a serene and pollution-free environment. Popular attractions include Panorama Point, Charlotte Lake, and the famous toy train ride that adds a nostalgic charm to the visit. Matheran is a perfect getaway for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

  1. Panorama Point - Offers a breathtaking 360-degree view of the surrounding valleys and sunrise.
  2. Charlotte Lake - A serene lake that serves as a picturesque picnic spot and a source of water for Matheran.
  3. Louisa Point - Known for its stunning views of waterfalls and nearby forts, making it a must-visit for photographers.
  4. Echo Point - Experience the fun of hearing your voice echo amidst the peaceful hills.
  5. The Toy Train Ride - A charming heritage train ride through lush green forests and scenic landscapes.
  • Vada Pav - A spicy and iconic street food of Maharashtra.
  • Chikki - A delicious brittle made of jaggery and nuts, perfect as a snack or souvenir.
  • Kanda Bhaji - Crispy onion fritters served with spicy chutney, ideal for a rainy day.
